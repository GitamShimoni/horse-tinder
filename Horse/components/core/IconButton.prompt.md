**IconButton** — round glass button for icon-only controls; the swipe deck uses `nope` / `love` / `super` tones for its X / heart / lightning actions.

```jsx
<IconButton icon="x" tone="nope" size="lg" label="Nope" />
<IconButton icon="heart" tone="love" size="lg" label="Like" />
<IconButton icon="zap" tone="super" label="Super Neigh" />
```

Tones: nope · like · love · super · neutral. Sizes: sm · md · lg. Hover lifts, scales, and blooms a colored glow ring.
